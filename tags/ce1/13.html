Project Management and Leadership Expo 2018

July 20, 2018

Halle 622

Zurich, Switzerland

WELCOME TO THE PROJECT MANAGEMENT AND LEADERSHIP EXPO 2018!

This premier event is the biggest gathering of change managers, which will provide an unprecedented opportunity for professional development and interactive learning. Attending this conference will help to discover creative solutions, stay current on innovative methods and tools and network with experienced colleagues and solution providers.

Discovering the trends, best practices and case studies will allow to get a better picture of where the industry is heading more effectively and take our programs to the next level for 2018 and beyond.

Attending PML Expo 2018 will allow to earn considerable learning credits toward obtaining/renewing the Certified Change Management Professional credential. This credential will not only help validate knowledge, experience and expertise around change management, but it will also be valuable to the company by adding credibility to the group and roles in this space.
